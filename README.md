## Game mechanics:

  - minimum player count is three, maximum is six
  - Card piles:

    - there is a temporary shared "trash" pile for all players available only
      during the present round where all cards are from the current round.
    - once the round is over all cards are reshuffled (Non-original rule).
    - there is also a shared "trick" stack, which is formed by cards played by 
      the players during each trick
    - at the end of each trick the cards in the trick stack will be given to the 
      winner of the trick

      - Tricks:
          - Rank order:
            (From highest to lowest): ace, king, queen, jack, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6, 5, 
            4, 3, 2
          - There are 5 tricks per round, with one trick being one cycle of play.
          - each player who participates in the trick may play one card from their hand
          - Rules which determine the player who initiates the trick:
              1. In the first round, the player left of the dealer (clockwise)
                is to go first
              2. If is not the first round but the first trick in a new round, the 
                previous round winner will go first
              3. If it not the first round or the first trick of a new round, the trick 
                initiater is determined by who won the last trick
          - During each cycle of each trick, players turns always go clockwise
          - the first player who begins the trick will determine the lead suit (defined below, link somehow) by placing one card down
          - Trump rule:
                if one or more cards of the trump suit are played, the highest-
                  ranked trump card wins that trick
          - Lead suit rule:
                - if a player has at least one card of the lead suit, they must play 
                  a card of said suit
                - if the player has no cards of the lead suit, they can play any suit of 
                  card
                - if there are no cards played of the trump suit by any player,
                  the highest-ranked card of the lead suit wins that trick, rewarding a trick point

          - a player may win a trick point if they played the highest trump card OR 
            if there was no trump card player by any other player, and they had
            the highest card of the lead suit
          - by winning a trick they collect that trick's cards and lead the next 
            trick
    - Folding:

      - a player may choose to fold once the round has started and the trump 
        card has been revealed 
      - a fold must be done before anyone completes their turn, to "knock" 
        means to stay in the round, to "pass" is to sit out the round
      - if a player folds their cards go into the shared pile to be discarded 
        for the round, no tricks may be participated in by a player who folded,
        for the duration of the round they folded in
      - you may not lose points if you have folded
    - Knocking:

        - this means you did not fold, and must play all tricks
        - the player may gain points or lose them
        - must be done in a clockwise order
        - if only one person knocks, they win that round
        - atleast one player must knock per round
    - to win the game, you must earn 10 total points
## Start of game:
  - shuffle cards
  - if the deck is ever empty (zero available cards to draw), reshuffle
  - the initial dealer is determined at random

 ## During the round:
  - if there are not enough cards to complete a full round, shuffle
  - if the first round, the player to the left of the dealer leads the first
    trick of said round
  - if not the first round, the dealer rotates clockwise after every round
  - every player is dealt five cards at once (5 to first, 5 to second, etc.)
  - the following card from the deck is dealt face up, this is the trump card,
    there is only one trump card dealt per round
  - players then choose to either fold/knock
  - cards may only be used in the same round they are dealt
  - there is only one deck ever in use at a time during the round
  - only these first five cards are dealt to each player during the round, 
    more dealing of cards never occurs, and players are to never draw cards during 
    the round
  - each round played consists of five tricks (original number of cards given)
  - the player with the most number of trick wins at the end of the round wins 
    the round, and recieves one point
  - any knocking player who has zero trick wins that round loses a point
  - all other knocking players neither gain nor lose points
